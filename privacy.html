Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Brenen's Restaurant Group ("we," "us") operates brenensrg.com. This policy describes what information we collect through this website and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect the information you provide: your name, email address, phone number, organization, area of interest, and message. We do not collect this information unless you choose to send it to us.

How we use it

We use contact-form submissions solely to respond to your inquiry about partnerships, catering, franchising, or other business with Brenen's Restaurant Group. We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for marketing lists without your consent.

Form processing

Contact-form submissions are delivered to us by a third-party form-processing service, which handles your submission in accordance with its own privacy policy.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not set advertising cookies. If we use basic analytics to understand site traffic, the data is aggregate and not used to identify individuals.

Data retention & your choices

We keep inquiry information only as long as needed to handle your request and related business follow-up. To ask us to correct or delete information you've sent us, contact any team member listed on our Contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date above reflects the latest revision.
