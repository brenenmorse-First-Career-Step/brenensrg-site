Caring Through Serving

Community

Local eateries are members of a larger community — and we are always looking for ways to give back.

Brenen's Restaurant Group knows that local eateries are part of a larger community, so we are always looking for ways to give back — whether through fundraisers or donations of food for special events. We are all in this together.

#Pizza4Providers

Part of Enzo's (a BRG company) and Brenz Chapel Hill's foodraising initiative — freely matching purchased donations. To date, over 2,500 pizzas have been donated to Duke Health and UNC Health.

Pelotonia

A weekend of cycling and volunteerism raising millions each year for cancer research at the OSU Comprehensive Cancer Center. Operating expenses are covered by partners like Brenz Pizza Co. and Brenen's Cafe, so 100% of every rider-raised dollar funds research.

Hoops for Hearing

Held each fall at the Women's Basketball Hall of Fame, Delta Zeta invites other greeks to a tournament raising funds for the Tennessee School for the Deaf. Brenz Knoxville donates pizza, and hosts fundraising nights giving 20% of sales back.
