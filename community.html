Caring Through Serving

Brenen's Restaurant Group knows that local eateries are members of a larger community, so we are always looking for ways to give back — whether through fundraisers or donations of food for special events. We are all in this together.

COVID-19 Response | Video ▶

Part of Enzo's (a BRG company) and Brenz Chapel Hill's #pizza4providers foodraising initiative was to freely match purchased donations. To date, over 2,500 pizzas have been donated to Duke Health and UNC Health.

Pelotonia | Video ▶

Pelotonia is a weekend of cycling, entertainment and volunteerism, raising millions of dollars each year for cancer research at The Ohio State University Comprehensive Cancer Center. Pelotonia's operating expenses are covered by partners and donated goods and services from companies such as Brenz Pizza Co. Columbus and Brenen's Cafe, so that 100% of every rider-raised dollar goes directly to fund cancer research.

Hoops for Hearing | Video ▶

Held each fall at the Women's Basketball Hall of Fame, Delta Zeta invites other greeks to a basketball tournament to raise funds for the Tennessee School for the Deaf. Brenz Knoxville participated by donating pizza for the event. Delta Zeta also invited other greeks to join them for an evening at Brenz, where 20% of the sales went to Delta Zeta's fundraising.
