Our Portfolio

Concepts & Cuisine

A portfolio spanning the most popular and innovative campus cuisines — each its own brand, each made fresh daily.

Brenen's Restaurant Group has developed a portfolio of concepts that bridge the gap between authenticity and familiarity. Using specialized chefs and endless market research, we develop menu offerings — and blend specialties into one efficient package — that thrive on campus.

Locotos

Latin American Grill

Locotos serves fresh, authentic Latin American cuisine in a familiar build-your-own bowl format. From hand-cut pico de gallo to in-house slow-roasted carnitas and barbacoa, every item is meticulously selected to create a unique culinary experience. (The evolution of our original Sazón concept.)

Visit Locotos →

Il Forno Kitchen

Italian Kitchen

Il Forno specializes in authentic Italian pastas, wood-fired artisan pizzas, and fresh vibrant salads. Fresh sauces, flavorful toppings, and great customer service combine into endless combinations of exciting meals — proudly serving campus communities since 2016.

Visit Il Forno →

Brenz Pizza Co

Handcrafted Pizza

Award-winning, handcrafted pizza built on dough made fresh on the premises and aged in-house for at least 24 hours. From house-made sauces to premium meats and fresh-sliced produce, every pie is meticulously prepared — plus jumbo wings, salads, subs, and wraps. A national Pizza Pizzazz competition standout since 2014.

Visit Brenz →

Breezy Bev Co

Coastal Refreshers

A coastal refresher company with a Nantucket soul — functional cold brews, fruit-forward coolers, tide tea lattes, swell soft serve and protein shakes. Order ahead, grab from the pickup wall, and go. Skip the line, live breezy.

Visit Breezy →

The Sub Hub

Submarine Sandwiches

Fresh-baked sub buns and sourdough baguettes, quality meats and cheeses, built to order. Ten signature subs available as a sub, a wrap, or a bowl — and every order comes with a side. Warmth, quality, craftsmanship.
